Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 06 June 2025

Polyglotter (“we,” “us,” or “our”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome Extension, website, and related services (“Services”).

By using Polyglotter, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. What We Collect

a. Data You Provide

  • Custom Word Uploads: If you upload your own vocabulary or personal dictionary, this data is stored securely and only accessible to your user account.

b. Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized data about extension usage (e.g., how often features are used, error logs) to improve performance.
  • Browser Environment: Basic technical information about your browser type, version, and extension version may be collected.

We do not collect or store:

  • Your browsing history
  • Personally identifying content from websites you visit
  • Sensitive personal information (e.g., passwords, financial data)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, personalize, and improve our Services
  • To allow you to upload, store, and manage your custom vocabulary
  • To respond to your support inquiries
  • To analyze usage trends and improve product functionality

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share anonymized usage data with third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve the product. We ensure these providers comply with strict data protection requirements.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption practices. We use secure cloud storage providers that comply with GDPR/CCPA standards.

You may delete your account and data at any time by contacting us.
